About Us

We are dedicated to providing timely alerts regarding air quality. Our website offers valuable information and resources to help individuals stay informed about air pollution.

Main Motive of AirQ

Our main motive is to send alerts through SMS when the air quality is poor. Additionally, our website provides news related to air pollution and offers a map view of air quality.

Modules of Our Website

  1. Air Quality Predictor

    The Air Quality Predictor shows whether the air quality is poor, good, moderate, or fair. It includes a table displaying concentration levels of various pollutants such as:

    Pollutant Concentration (μg/m³) Level
    Carbon Monoxide 480.65 Poor
    Nitrogen Monoxide 0.13 Fair
    Nitrogen Dioxide 23.99 Moderate
    Ozone 8.4 Fair
    Sulphur Dioxide 21.94 Moderate
    Fine Particles Matter 11.92 Good
    Coarse Particulate Matter 18.59 Moderate
    Ammonia 5.19 Fair

  2. Alert Messages

    We send alert messages to notify users about the air quality status. Depending on the air quality level, we provide proactive measures to help users stay safe and minimize their exposure to pollution.

  3. Air Quality Product Showcase

    Our website features a showcase of air quality products designed to help individuals and communities improve air quality and reduce pollution.

  4. Map View of Air Quality

    We offer a map view displaying real-time air quality data, allowing users to visualize air pollution levels in their area.

  5. News Related to Air Quality

    Stay updated with the latest news and information related to air quality, environmental issues, and efforts to combat pollution.
